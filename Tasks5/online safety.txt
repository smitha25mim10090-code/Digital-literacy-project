# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

## ✅ Everyday Safety Habits

* ☐ **Use strong, unique passwords**
  Create different passwords for each app (especially email, banking, and social media). Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

* ☐ **Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
  Turn on 2FA for apps like email, Instagram, and banking to add an extra security layer.

* ☐ **Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks**
  Do not log in to banking or payment apps on public Wi-Fi in cafés, hostels, or campuses.

* ☐ **Don’t click unknown links**
  Avoid links from unknown emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages—even if they look “official” or urgent.

* ☐ **Download apps only from trusted sources**
  Use Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Never install APK files sent on WhatsApp or Telegram.

* ☐ **Limit personal information online**
  Don’t share phone number, address, college ID, or daily routine publicly on social media.

---

## 💸 UPI & Financial Safety (Very Important)

* ☐ **Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money**
  You only need a PIN to **send** money—not to receive it.

* ☐ **Check payment requests carefully**
  Read before approving “collect requests” on apps like PhonePe, Google Pay, etc.

* ☐ **Never share OTP, PIN, or CVV with anyone**
  Banks or payment apps will **never ask** for these details.

* ☐ **Verify before buying/selling online**
  Be cautious on OLX, Instagram shops, or Telegram sellers—avoid advance payments to unknown sellers.

---

## 📱 Device & App Security

* ☐ **Keep your phone and apps updated**
  Updates fix security vulnerabilities and protect against new threats.

* ☐ **Use screen lock & app lock**
  Protect your phone with fingerprint, PIN, or face lock to prevent unauthorized access.

* ☐ **Log out from shared devices**
  Always log out if using a friend’s or college computer.

---

## 🚨 If Something Goes Wrong

* ☐ **Report immediately (within minutes!)**
  Quick reporting increases chances of recovering money.

  👉 **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
  👉 **Helpline Number:** 📞 1930

* ☐ **Inform your bank/payment app instantly**
  Block transactions or freeze your account if needed.

---

## 🎯 Quick Reminder

✔ Think before you click
✔ Verify before you trust
✔ Act fast if scammed
